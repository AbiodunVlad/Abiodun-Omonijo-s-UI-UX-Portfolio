Service U


The Challenge

It becomes increasingly difficult to find efficient service providers in certain fields, and probably at close range. Even when they are close, we still have difficulty finding them especially in new areas.

The Goal

The goal is that everyone is able to find capable hands to do business with in their neighborhood, everyday.

The Solution

The solution to the problem as stated above is to create a marketplace where domestic and technical service providers can put up their interest to offer their service, and where those who need them can request for their service. Service providers and clients are then automatically matched based on their location and requirements.

FFO Mart


The Challenge

With modern life and the problems working-class people have purchasing food items due to the lack of sufficient time, they need a reliable and consistent source of supply for their consumables, and one that can meet their needs without having to break the bank. Importantly, shoppers need to be able to make orders seamlessly and in a fun way.

The Goal

The goal was to design an interface for a mobile application where users can easily (pre)order various food items where they are categorised according to their types.

The Solution

I came up with a world-class type of B2C mobile product that is easy and fun to use for every user, giving a lead on what steps to take for a seamless transaction through a simple user flow so everyone can have a fantastic user experience at the end of each transaction.

The Challenge
